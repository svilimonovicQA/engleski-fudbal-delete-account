Account and Data Deletion – Engleski Fudbal

This page explains how users can request account and data deletion for the Engleski Fudbal app.

Developer/App

Engleski Fudbal / Engleski Fudbal

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to: engleski.fudbal@gmail.com with subject: Delete my account
  2. Include the email address used in the app and your User ID (UID), if available.
  3. We will confirm your request and process it within 30 days.

What is currently deleted when we process an account deletion request

User profile data from Firestore users/{uid} including:

  • email
  • displayName
  • username
  • soccerUsername
  • favoriteClub
  • createdAt
  • isVerified
  • profilePhotoUrl (if present)

Profile photo from Firebase Storage path:

  • users/{uid}/profile.jpg

Firebase Authentication account (email/password credentials).

What may be retained (current app architecture)

  • Quiz attempt and leaderboard-related records currently stored in Firestore collection userQuizAttempts may remain unless explicitly removed by backend/admin deletion workflow.
  • Logs maintained by platform providers (Firebase/Google Play infrastructure) may be retained for fraud prevention, abuse prevention, legal, and operational purposes.

Retention period

  • Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
  • Any legally required retention is kept only for the minimum required period under applicable law/platform policy, then deleted.

Important transparency note

The current mobile app does not yet expose an in-app “Delete Account” button.

Account/data deletion is currently handled via support request email.

Ova stranica objašnjava kako korisnici mogu da zatraže brisanje naloga i podataka za aplikaciju Engleski Fudbal.

Programer/Aplikacija

Engleski Fudbal / Engleski Fudbal

Kako zatražiti brisanje

  1. Pošaljite email na: engleski.fudbal@gmail.com sa naslovom: Delete my account
  2. Uključite email adresu korišćenu u aplikaciji i vaš korisnički ID (UID), ako je dostupan.
  3. Potvrdićemo vaš zahtev i obraditi ga u roku od 30 dana.

Šta se trenutno briše kada obradimo zahtev za brisanje naloga

Podaci korisničkog profila iz Firestore users/{uid} uključuju:

  • email
  • displayName
  • username
  • soccerUsername
  • favoriteClub
  • createdAt
  • isVerified
  • profilePhotoUrl (ako postoji)

Profilna fotografija iz Firebase Storage putanje:

  • users/{uid}/profile.jpg

Firebase Authentication nalog (email/lozinka kredencijali).

Šta može biti zadržano (trenutna arhitektura aplikacije)

  • Zapisi o pokušajima kviza i leaderboard podaci koji se trenutno čuvaju u Firestore kolekciji userQuizAttempts mogu ostati osim ako nisu eksplicitno uklonjeni kroz backend/admin workflow brisanja.
  • Logovi koje održavaju platform provajderi (Firebase/Google Play infrastruktura) mogu biti zadržani zbog sprečavanja prevara, zloupotreba, pravnih i operativnih razloga.

Period zadržavanja

  • Zahtevi za brisanje se obrađuju u roku od 30 dana.
  • Svako zakonski obavezno zadržavanje podataka čuva se samo minimalno neophodan period prema važećem zakonu/politici platforme, a zatim se briše.

Važna napomena o transparentnosti

Trenutna mobilna aplikacija još uvek nema in-app dugme “Delete Account”.

Brisanje naloga/podataka se trenutno rešava putem email zahteva podršci.
